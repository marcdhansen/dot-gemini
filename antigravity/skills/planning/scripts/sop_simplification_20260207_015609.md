## Template Usage Instructions

### For Agents
1. **Complete all sections thoroughly** - Incomplete proposals will be rejected
2. **Be specific about justifications** - Vague reasoning is insufficient
3. **Focus on quality maintenance** - Explain how quality outcomes are preserved
4. **Assess risks honestly** - Underestimating risks leads to rejection

### For Users
1. **Review quality gates section carefully** - Ensure critical requirements maintained
2. **Evaluate risk-benefit analysis** - Consider both efficiency gains and potential risks
3. **Check success criteria** - Ensure clear metrics for evaluation
4. **Document reasoning** - Clear approval/rejection reasoning helps future decisions

### Post-Implementation
1. **Update retrospective** - Include evaluation of simplification effectiveness
2. **Create Beads issue** - If pattern should be available for future tasks
3. **Document lessons learned** - Share successful patterns with team
